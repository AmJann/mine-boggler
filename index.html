Mine Boggler

Make your way from start to finish with the highest score possible.

You will come across different objects here's a list of what they are and what they do.

  • Bombs: If you click on a space with a bomb you lose 500 points
  • Stars: if you click on a space with a Star you gain 500 points
  • Question Marks: Take your chance it could be mildly good or bad for your score. ALso, it could be greatly bad or good for your score
  • Blocks: You can't move to these spaces they "block" your way!

Play repeatedly to try and beat the highscore!
